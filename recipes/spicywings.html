Spicy Wings

Spicy Chicken Wings are always a fan favorite

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 20 Chicken Wings
  • 1/2 Cup Melted Butter
  • 1/2 Cup Frank's Red Hot

Steps

  1. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil, and lightly grease with cooking spray.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  3. While the oven is preheating, whisk together melted butter and hot sauce in a small bowl. Dip wings into hot sauce mixture, and place back on the baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Flip wings and continue to cook until crispy on the outside and no longer pink in the center (about 25 more minutes).
