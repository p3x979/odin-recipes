Waffles

Delicious Waffles are a tasty breakfast treat that are simple to make

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 3/4 Cups Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Coconut Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon Brown Sugar
  • 4 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron.
  2. Whisk eggs in a large bowl until fluffy. Add flour, milk, and vegetable oil and mix to combine. Whisk in brown sugar, then mix in baking powder, cinamon, salt, and vanilla being careful not to overmix.
  3. Pour batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook until golden brown. 3 to 5 minutes.
